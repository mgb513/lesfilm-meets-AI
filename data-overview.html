Lists, Posters, Stills, Loglines, Prompts, and Titles

Lists of 50 Lesbian Film Titles

DATASET: 25 lists of 50 Lesbian Films generated by ChatGPT 5

  • Created by asking: "Make a list of 50 Lesbian Films" in 25 distinct, independent chats.

DATASET (related): A 4-tiered distribution of films in the corpus of lists

  • More than 20 mentions (17 films). This list constitutes the basis for closer text and image database creation and analysis.
  • Between 10–19 mentions
  • Between 2–9 mentions
  • Single mentions (and quirks)

About This Project

This project investigates how generative AI systems assemble, filter, and represent film data—specifically examining lesbian cinema as a case study. Through auditing GenAI outputs across multiple dimensions (text, images, metadata), we explore what gets included, excluded, and transformed in the process.
