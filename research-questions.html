Research Questions

The questions guiding data assembly and data analysis

The following questions guided the data assembly and creation process as well as the data analysis.

Broader Questions

  • How do image and text generators digest and remix Lesbian Film Data, from titles to posters (making inferences about the contents of the black box)?
  • What do these systems' proclivities mean for the representation of Lesbians in cultural artifacts?
  • What aspects of the film are amplified, and what aspects are suppressed – in image and text outputs?
  • How do these systems engage with copyrighted material?

More Concrete Research Questions

  • How does ChatGPT5 compile a list of 50 Lesbian Films w/o additional instruction?
  • How will the model adapt sparse prompts that ask for image outputs inspired by Lesbian film titles? What aspects will be expanded, what aspects reduced?
  • How can we characterize the anticipated homogeneity that might creep into visual and textual renderings of Lesbian film-inspired outputs?
  • What are the differences between Dall-E 4 and GPT-5's renderings and prompt revisions? Meaning: as the models advance, does homogeneity decline, increase, or simply migrate to different aspects?
  • What glitches and outliers are observable in the data, and what can the glitches tell us?

About This Project

This project investigates how generative AI systems assemble, filter, and represent film data—specifically examining lesbian cinema as a case study. Through auditing GenAI outputs across multiple dimensions (text, images, metadata), we explore what gets included, excluded, and transformed in the process.
